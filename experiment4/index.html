Experiment 4 - Images, Video, & Sound Art

Imitate

As soon as I heard we can use video images and sound I thought about this meme: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdi19AZvHkQ

It’s a funny meme with a gif of a cute girl from the video game A Hat in Time dancing to “Aaron Smith Danci n- (KRONO Remix)” while the background is changing. I wanted to first recreate this and maybe add different backgrounds instead.

Integrate

I mostly took what we learned in class about adding images and videos and made a gif of the girl dancing while an array of videos loop through in the background.

I also looped music in the assignment and the most annoying part was rescaling and repositioning the girl each time the scene changes.

Innovate

Finally, made it different from the original video by having the brightness change to fit the background.

I also added a webcam in the middle of the videos.

Reflection

Each person that worked on the code should reflect on the process, the difficulties, and the successes of the experiment. Here's where you can put your reflections.

  • Although this work is not impressive at all and on paper pretty easy, it took too long.
  • I think I captured the essence of the original video and I had to find my own clips and edit each down to upload to github. I think the biggest challenge was spending time finding videos to use clips in the background and the tedious amount of work put into matching the girl with the background.
  • My highest point was definitely finally experiencing the whole sequence of video, or the final product myself. I hope you enjoy watching and listening to it!
