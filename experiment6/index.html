Experiment 6 - Grammars & Text Art

Imitate

I wanted to make an insult generator because I remember looking at one called "Shakespeare Insult Generator."

link:https://codepo8.github.io/shakespeare-insult-generator/

I wanted to make mine seem more animated and instead of Shakspeare I mostly asked chatGPT fpr a word bank of insulting nouns, verbs and adjectives."

Integrate

After learning about the basics of the Tracery library from class and also the official github page, it was very easy for me to definte the rules and grammar.

I could generate random insults now in the form of "You are one #adjective# #noun# that likes to #verb#".

I also needed to animate the insult words moving down like casino slots too and I borrowed most of my code from here:https://editor.p5js.org/ffmaer/sketches/odU88zEVV.

Innovate

Finally, the hardest part was actually getting the insult generated and displayed by tracery and the casino slot words to line up.

Because I could not figure out how flatten can access each individual type of obj in the Json file to give to the user, I decided it was best that I parse through the already flattened output and try to grab the words.

I then made it so each slot, representing the noun, adjective and verb would stop to the word that was generated."
